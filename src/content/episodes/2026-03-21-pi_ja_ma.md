This morning, we have the immense joy of welcoming [Pi Ja Ma](https://pi-ja-ma.bandcamp.com/album/magnetofille) as our guest. She shares with us some of her best gems that will surely put a smile on your face and accompany you on a Sunday made just for you. Let the music play, enjoy a comforting breakfast as the vibes take you, and let's go for a walk all day guided only by music and the deep feeling of presence that emanates from the simple things around you.

# Pi Ja Ma's selection

## Brigitte Fontaine et Areski Belkacem - C’est normal

Pi Ja Ma: **"** Cette chanson est très drôle mais il faut comprendre le français ! Si on ne comprend pas le français, c’est pas grave parce qu’elle est aussi très entrainante mais quand même assez calme pour un réveil en douceur. Si vous voulez expliquer ce que sont « des français » à des aliens, je trouve que c’est le bon morceau. **"** 

## [Rouge Gorge](https://dokidoki.bandcamp.com/album/ren) - Allez-vous en

Pi Ja Ma: **"** Pour passer un bon dimanche (selon moi), il faut dire à tout le monde de nous laisser tranquille, enclencher une boîte à rythme et danser en culotte dans sa cuisine en faisant du café. Rouge Gorge fait cette musique très simple qui me touche et ne me lasse jamais. **"** 

## [Miki](https://mikipedia.fr/) - Ça pik un peu quand même

Pi Ja Ma: **"** Impossible de ne pas bouger son épaule sur ce tube de Miki. Une belle chanson pour se consoler de sa friendzone du moment. Miki c’est l’artiste pop la plus fun du moment. Elle s’amuse et se fout de ce qu’on pense d’elle et ça fait du bien de crier « nanananana » avec elle. **"** 

# ImaCrea's selection

## [Pi Ja Ma](https://pi-ja-ma.bandcamp.com/album/magnetofille) - Chiale

ImaCrea: **"** I discovered Pi Ja Ma with this track; [the video is great](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=81e-_rF6AFk). I instantly loved the atmosphere and the joyous vibe she brings to her music. The softness of her voice and the apparent innocence of the childlike and simple melodies contrast with her light-hearted lyrics about vulnerability, failure, and the disconnection of our all-digital, individualistic era; the result is perfectly balanced. Her debut album *[Magnetofille](https://pi-ja-ma.bandcamp.com/album/magnetofille)* is a beautiful gem. It feels like a new genre that could only have emerged in recent years, a truly contemporary and inspiring work that invites us to remember that joy and love remain, and always will be, our best remedy for hope. **"** 

## [Alice](https://lesdisquesbongojoe.bandcamp.com/album/futur-simple-bongo-joe-10-years) - La fête est nulle

ImaCrea: **"** Friday night, driving in Paris, barely seeing through the rain-streaked glass. His heart is heavy; I guess he wishes this brief trip could have lasted longer, but now it's time to go back home. I put Pi Ja Ma on the radio, and then Pierre-Benjamin suggests this track. It matched the moment perfectly, and we arrived home with smiles on our faces. **"** 

## [World Brain](https://worldbrain.bandcamp.com/album/open-source) - Minute papillon

ImaCrea: **"** Just been a while since I last listened to World Brain, whom I was so happy to invite on our [episode 435](https://www.mailta.pe/435/world-brain/) back in 2021. His latest album, *[Open Source](https://worldbrain.bandcamp.com/album/open-source)*, is a great joy I definitely recommend. This track comes from it, and I felt it was perfect to keep the vibe of this selection. I love Noémie Chantre’s voice and these poetic lyrics. **"** 

## [Musique Chienne, Earth Dad](https://musiquechienne.bandcamp.com/album/ending) - Regular Times

ImaCrea: **"** And so I remembered about Musique Chienne's music, whom Inès invited back in 2018 for our [296th episode](https://www.mailta.pe/296/musique-chienne/). We had the chance back then to invite her to a MailTape Live session on Le Barboteur's boat in Pantin, a great moment. And so I discovered this recent work I could find on her [bandcamp's profile](https://musiquechienne.bandcamp.com/album/ending) and I love these new explorations! Wish there was more to listen to. **"** 

That's all folks! Thanks for being with us <3 A great and warm thank to Pi Ja Ma for her lovely selection and music.
