This morning, we are thrilled to feature **Sessa** as our guest. Based in **São Paulo**, he's curated a selection infused with beautiful, warm vibes from across the ocean. I discovered his work through **Tiago Caetano's selection** in [episode 562](https://www.mailta.pe/562/tiago-caetano/) back in **2024**, and I have been following Sessa's music with immense pleasure ever since.

He recently released his latest album, *[Pequena Vertigem de Amor](https://sessa.bandcamp.com/album/pequena-vertigem-de-amor)*, which we wholeheartedly recommend. So come on, let the sunshine in, push away the clouds that blur your imagination, and immerse yourself in these uplifting musical vibes.

# Guest's selection

## Gal Costa - The Archaic Lonely Star

Sessa: **"** When putting this song on a sunday morning you might have the privilege of not knowing if you're awake or still dreaming in the sound of the first guitar chords by Lanny Gordin. **"** 

## Jim Huxley - Tessa On a Magazine

Sessa: **"** I just love this stoned sunny vibe that comes out from the speakers when you play this song. Good way to start a slow day. **"** 

## Hailu Mergia & Dahlak Band - Anchin Kfu Ayinkash

Sessa: **"** Now that we're three songs in, it feels like we have enough energy to kick in with some mellow dance moves. I love this recording in how they convey so much emotion with an instrumental tune and how things sound otherworldly bizarre. **"**
